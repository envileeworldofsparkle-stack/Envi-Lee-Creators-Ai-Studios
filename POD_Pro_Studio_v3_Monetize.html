Pricing Recommendation
Pricing psychology
Charm pricing works
$34.99 converts better than $35. The brain reads the first digit — it sees $34, not $35.
Price signals quality
Too cheap hurts you. If your luxury tee is $12, customers assume it's cheap. Price up.
The golden margin rule
Target 40–60% margin minimum. Below 30% and you can't run ads profitably.
Anchor with bundles
Offer a $60 2-piece bundle. Suddenly your $35 single item feels like a deal.
