FAQ & Support
Quick answers to the most common questions.
Frequently asked questions
How do I generate my first mockup?
Open POD Pro Studio → click Mockup Generator in the sidebar → choose your product type → upload your design or use the sketch tool → click Generate. It takes about 30 seconds.
What are credits and how do they work?
Credits are used every time you generate something with AI. Text generations cost 1 credit. Mockups cost 5 credits. Your Boss Plan includes 1,500 credits per month, which renews on the 1st of each month.
How do I connect Printify to my store?
In POD Pro Studio → go to POD Integration Hub → click Printify → follow the connection steps. You'll need a free Printify account. Once connected, you can push products directly from the app.
When does the live coaching happen?
Live coaching sessions happen every Tuesday at 7PM EST inside the Baddie Academy Skool community. If you can't make it live, all sessions are recorded and available in the community resource library.
How does the affiliate program work?
Share your unique link (found under Affiliate → My Referral Link). Every person who signs up through your link and becomes a paying member earns you 30% commission every month they stay. Tracked by a 90-day cookie. Paid monthly on the 15th.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
Yes — no contracts, cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. If you cancel, you keep your content and saved prompts.
